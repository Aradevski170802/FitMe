Body Mass Index Calculator (BMI)

The body mass index (BMI) calculator is a tool that helps determine an individual's body fat based on their weight and height. The BMI is calculated by dividing an individual's weight in kilograms by their height in meters squared (kg/mÂ²). A person with a BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered to be at a healthy weight, while a BMI below 18.5 is considered underweight and a BMI above 25 is considered overweight. However, it's important to note that BMI is not a perfect measure of body fat, as it doesn't take into account factors such as muscle mass or overall body composition. Therefore, it's important to use other methods, such as skinfold thickness measurements or bioelectrical impedance analysis, to determine body fat percentage.
